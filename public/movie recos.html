Ginnie's Movie Guide

My best movie recommendations of all time.

Fair Play

I loved Emily's character. She's strong, independent and a goal-getter but also loves genuinely. I loved how, in the end, she handled herself gracefully despite being hurt and betrayed by a narcissistic man.

Finding Nemo

Loved the songs! This movie always brings me back my childhood memories. This is the first movie that taught me to never give up on what or who you love.

50 First Dates

I'm a romcom addict and this one's my all time favorite. It is funny, romantic, the actors are suberp and the setting is a dream.
